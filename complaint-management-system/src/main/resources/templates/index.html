The pipeline

From "I have a problem" to "it's being fixed."

Every complaint moves through the same four checkpoints, whether it arrived by form or by phone call.

01

Intake

Complaints arrive from email, chat, a web form, WhatsApp, or a transcribed call — all logged the same way.

02

Prioritize

AI reads the complaint and scores it — a safety issue never waits behind a routine query.

03

Route

Based on what the complaint is actually about, it's assigned to the correct department automatically.

04

Resolve

Agents track every status change on a timeline, until the complaint is closed and the record is complete.
